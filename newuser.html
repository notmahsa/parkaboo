Why Parkaboo?


On average, we spend about up to 14 minutes a day looking for a spot each time we park.




It might not seem like much, but it adds up.

That's 70 minutes a week. 5 hours a month. 60 hours a year.




At Parkaboo, we're here to give you that time back so you can spend it with people you love.




We combine proven, reliable software and cutting edge innovation by combining Google Maps with our own parking prediction algorithmns.



This technology enables us to precisely and accurately tell you when and where you can park. Conveniently and free of charge.



We even reward our users for their help, as we recognize that their time and effort is valuable to us.



By registering as a user, you help us and your fellow users improve our prediction services. We promise we'll deliver only the best to you and protect your information like it's our own.
