30%

Of all cars in major cities are looking for parking at any given time.





This statistic has many drastic consequences on traffic throughput and congestion.









3.5 to 14 Minutes

Average time spent by a driver searching for an open parking spot.




10 to 20 Minutes

Time lost per mile (~1.6km) by through traffic due to drivers circulating for parking.




200 to 300%

Increase in travel time caused by additional congestion.







Cost of parking, limited availability and hefty parking fines are also problems faced by urban residents.






Parkaboo aims to address the lack of a elegant solution to finding open and free parking spaces in crowded cities. Built on the Google Maps API and using crowd sourced data, Parkaboo provides real-time updates on unoccupied spots in lots, garages, and streets with free parking.








15/09/2017

Parkaboo was born!





Parkaboo was created by Mahsa Naserifar and Joseph Choy at Hack the North 2017.
What is Parkaboo?

Parkaboo uses crowd-sourced data to find the closest unoccupied free parking space, helping you get to where you're going.
